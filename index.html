The Journey Behind XO for Software

XO for Software began as a focused team in 2024 and, after a series of meaningful achievements, grew into a startup company officially established in November 2025. Our team brings together a wide range of IT skills to build modern web, mobile & AI solutions. We move with a clear vision: to contribute to the region’s digital transformation and create products that deliver real value to businesses and communities.
